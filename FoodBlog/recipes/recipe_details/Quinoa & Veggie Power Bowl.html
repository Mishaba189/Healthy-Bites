Quinoa & Veggie Power Bowl

A colorful, protein-packed bowl made with quinoa, fresh vegetables, and healthy fats ‚Äî perfect for a quick and nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  7. 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. 1 tbsp lemon juice

How to Make

  • Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool slightly.
  • Rinse and drain chickpeas.
  • Chop all vegetables evenly.
  • In a large bowl, add quinoa, chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, and corn.
  • Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Mix gently and top with avocado slices.
  • Serve fresh or chilled.
