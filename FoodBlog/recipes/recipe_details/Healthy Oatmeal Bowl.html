Healthy Oatmeal Bowl

A warm, comforting, and nutrient-rich oatmeal bowl topped with fresh fruits, crunchy nuts, and natural sweetness ‚Äî perfect for a quick, healthy breakfast or post-workout meal.

Ingredients

  1. ¬Ω cup rolled oats
  2. 1 cup milk or plant-based milk (almond/oat)
  3. 1 small banana (sliced)
  4. ¬º cup blueberries
  5. 1 tbsp mixed nuts (walnuts/almonds, chopped)
  6. 1 tsp chia seeds
  7. 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  8. ¬º tsp cinnamon (optional)
  9. Pinch of salt

How to Make

  • In a saucepan, bring milk to a gentle boil.
  • Add oats and a pinch of salt.
  • Cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until creamy.
  • Turn off heat and pour oatmeal into a bowl.
  • Top with banana slices, blueberries, nuts, and chia seeds.
  • Drizzle honey or maple syrup on top.
  • Sprinkle cinnamon if desired and serve warm.
